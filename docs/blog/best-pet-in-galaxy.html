The Best Pet in All the Galaxy

Morph was me trusted sidekick and pet, before I handed him over to Jimbo. He’s known for being quite mischievous and has caused trouble in the past. He displayed a liking to Jimbo and assisted him with his morphing powers.

After Treasure Planet, I tried to get me Morph back, but he seemed to have grown so fond of the boy that he cried himself into a puddle! So I let Morph stay with Jimbo. Even though I miss the little guy, I know he’s in a good place with Jimbo at his mother’s inn on Montressor.
