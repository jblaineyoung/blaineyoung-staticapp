Davy Jones

Davy Jones was the supernatural ruler of the Seven Seas as the condemned captain of the Flying Dutchman. A fearsome and ruthless captain, striking terror into the bravest of sailors, Jones became the stuff of various myths and legends of Pirate Lore. He now uses his spare times writing and taking pictures of his pet Kraken. He has clips in Pirate Cove and The Daily Courant.
